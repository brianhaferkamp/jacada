Fortune 50 Manufacturer Transforms Customer Service with Multiexperience Capabilities

How to scale your customer service assets across B2C and B2B2C channels?

Leverage a low-code multiexperience toolkit to rapidly build and launch customer service apps and intelligent virtual agents.

The Client

GE is headquartered in the northeast United States. With over 100 years of operation, the company has grown its operations globally. Operations of this magnitude can often warrant high traffic flowing into the customer service support center. This customer engaged Jacada to improve the customer and retailer experience and reduce the inbound call volume.

The Approach

To enhance the customer and retailer experience and reduce the inbound call volume, GE decided to leverage Jacada Interact to build a Multiexperience that spans multiple channels and modalities to deliver a consistent customer experience (CX). The client, together with Jacada, targeted the following customer service contact types to develop a guided self-service experience with omnichannel access:

  • WISMO for Major Appliances
  • Schedule Home Installations
  • Schedule Technician Visits
  • Follow-up Inquiries
  • Web, IVR & Email touchpoints
  • Escalate to Live Assistance with context

The Benefits

Noticeable increase in the preference of their customers to self-service and reduced customer and retailer effort

Able to leverage existing support assets from their website in developing the guided servicing experience

Could offer additional capabilities like a visual tap and text, callbacks and natural language processing (NLP) for improved intent recognition.

What's Next?

Using the granular reporting and analytics provided by Jacada’s customer service automation platform, GE is finding additional opportunities to boost digital adoption and acting on those opportunities with precision and agility.
